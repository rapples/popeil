The Popeal is Right Game (The Price is Right)



Rules: Each player bids in order and must bid as close as they can to the actual retail price without going over.
Contestants bid in dollars and cents as the retail prices are not rounded off, and may not bid the same amount as any player bid previously for that prize.


Instructions: Click on the video to play it. Click the Next Video button to advance through the videos.
After each product is shown try to guess the price.

Alternate who guesses first and keep score!
The closest guess gets 1 point. Game is 12 points long.
